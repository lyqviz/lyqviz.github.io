by Yuqing  |  March, 2019

This data visualization looks at which countries have won the most top prizes at film festivals. Some film festivals have multiple winners in certain years, while some do not hold competitions every year.

According to the FIAPF (International Federation of Film Producers Associations), there are 15 accredited Competitive Feature Film Festivals. 9 of them are from Europe, 3 film festivals are from Asia, the rest are from North America, South America and Africa.
