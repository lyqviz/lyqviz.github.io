Hi, thanks for visiting my website! I'm Yuqing, a graduate student in the Information Design and Data Visualization Program at Northeastern University in Boston. I'm currently working at Business Insider as a graphics fellow, before that, I worked at NBC's national newsroom as a visual graphic intern in New York.

What excites me the most? Data-driven art, visualization and information design - anything that involves storytelling and a technical challenge, I'm into. I use many different tools, libraries, and programming languages to produce my works, including D3.js (and other JavaScript libraries, like Leaflet.js), Python, R, ArcGIS/QGIS, Illustrator and pencils.
