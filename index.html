Hi, thanks for visiting my website! I'm Yuqing, a graduate student in the Information Design and Visualization Program at Northeastern University in Boston. Before that, I studied data journalism at Columbia Journalism School.

What excites me the most? Data-driven art, visualization and information design - anything that involves storytelling and a technical challenge, I'm into. I use many different tools, libraries, and programming languages to produce my works, including D3.js (and other JavaScript libraries, like Leaflet.js), Python, R, ArcGIS/QGIS, Illustrator and pencils.

Outside of my work, I'm an avid cook, baker, and lifelong student of Japanese culture. Hirokazu Kore-eda is my favorite Japanese director whose narrativies always present a heartrending glimpse into an often invisible segment of society or family. If you love him, hotpot or cats, we could probably be friends.
