Food Insecurity in Boston Neighborhoods

This is a bubble visualization comparing neighborhoods in Boston along three variables: population, number of food insecure individuals, and unemployment rate. The circles are sized according to population, and the shade of the circles correspond to food insecurity. Darker circles have more food insecure individuals, and lighter ones have less.
Click the buttons to arrange the neighborhoods by unemployment rate.
